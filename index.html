Happy 37th Monthsary 💙

To My Love

A letter from my heart to yours

Happy 37th monthsary, my love! Even after the escalation of the situation that happened, we are still here continuing our journey together.

First, I’m really sorry for the sudden decision I made. At that moment, I felt like I could no longer endure everything and thought it was the only way. I realize now that I was wrong for that sudden outburst and for putting both of us in a situation where we were hurting.

I couldn’t fully process what happened at first, but it became clear to me that you were willing to fight for us. Honestly, everything you did and said, even within those few days, had a huge impact on me. It helped me realize that there is still something worth holding on to.

I don’t want you to question yourself or blame everything on you, because that’s not the point moving forward. We both had our faults, but now we also have our realizations, to continue cherishing what we have and to value our relationship even more.

It means so much to receive so much love from you. Thank you, truly, because you are the best. You’re not doing anything wrong, you’re simply loving me, and I feel that deeply.

I don’t want to be selfish by not giving that same love back to you, because you deserve to feel the love I have for you as well. I love you, and my love for you never changed. What happened in the past doesn’t change how I feel about you.

I’m beyond grateful for all your efforts and your words. Let’s continue our love, leave the past behind, and not let it hold us back. Instead, let’s move forward together and create more beautiful memories.

I love you more than you’ll ever know, my missy love. ❤️
